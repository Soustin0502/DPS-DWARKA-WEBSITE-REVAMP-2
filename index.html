MISSION:
Delhi Public School, Dwarka offers a well-balanced multidimensional curriculum rich in academic and co-curricular activities in an inclusive and diverse environment. Our educational ideology is based on the flowering of an individual's latent talents, development of creativity and imagination, intellectual curiosity and socio-moral responsibility. We are dedicated to cultivating students who are positive and productive members of a global society.
VISION:
DPS Dwarka is strategically poised to provide high-quality rigorous educational experience to all. This will be achieved through in-depth and scientific planning, research and action, inching towards excellence for all.
CORE PHILOSOPHY:

"Children are like cherubs sent by Him to beautify this colossal world It is their innocence and loving deeds that give us a glimpse of paradise To prevent the fall of this paradise is our responsibility To equip them with best education, great values and beliefs is our duty"
The core philosophy of DPS Dwarka is its endeavour to instill a value system in each individual, so as to help him withstand all the tests of time. We believe that true education is training of both the head & the heart. Academic excellence is desirable but inculcating good values is the essence of education.
We take upon the task of not only raising career conscious students but also socially responsible and global citizens. We aim to impart holistic education to ensure that our children grow up as individuals who are morally upright, emotionally balanced, culturally integrated, socially aware and spiritually oriented.

It is a matter of immense pride that Mr V.K.Shunglu, Hon'ble Chairman, The Delhi Public School Society, has been conferred with the prestigious Padma Bhushan award.
